About LARK International

Our Company

LARK International Co., LTD, established in Taiwan, is a creative textile company that offers knitting and woven fabric according to the request of performance, specification, and colors.


Carrying out research and development on advanced materials, we are aiming to combine our technologies with the customers to accelerate development and achieve more efficient result.


We apply trend concepts to fabric for sportswear, yoga wear, protective clothing, and other special garment. Quality fabric, competitive price, refined details, and customer satisfaction are our persistence.

What We Do

LARK implements the ideas through yarn selection / innovation, and fabric construction. Based on market conditions, we keep developing advanced materials and technology of functional fibers / composite materials.
